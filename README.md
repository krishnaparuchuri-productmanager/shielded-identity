# 🛡️ FraudShield

**AI-powered fraud detection and identity verification for 500 million Indians.**

FraudShield is a mobile-first application that helps banks, lenders, fintechs, and KYC teams instantly verify Aadhaar-linked identities, detect fraud signals in real time, and manage suspicious cases — all from a single, easy-to-use console.

---

## 🌍 The Problem We Solve

Identity fraud in India is growing faster than ever:

- **₹30,000+ crore** is lost annually to digital identity fraud
- **1 in 4** loan applications shows signs of synthetic or stolen identity
- SIM swaps, document tampering, and OTP bypass attacks are increasingly common
- KYC teams are overwhelmed with manual reviews and slow verification cycles

Traditional KYC tools verify *who* a person says they are. **FraudShield verifies whether you should trust them.**

---

## ✨ What FraudShield Does

### 🔍 Instant Identity Scan
Enter an Aadhaar number and get an AI-driven risk score in seconds. The system scans for 8+ fraud signals including SIM swaps, duplicate identities, address mismatches, biometric anomalies, and velocity fraud.

### 📊 Smart Risk Scoring
Every identity is scored from **0 to 100** and classified as **Low**, **Medium**, **High**, or **Critical** risk — with an AI confidence rating so analysts know how much to trust the result.

### ✅ Guided KYC Onboarding
A simple, step-by-step verification flow takes new users through Aadhaar OTP, document upload, liveness check, and final review — designed to feel as easy as opening a bank account on your phone.

### 🗂️ Analyst Dashboard
A central case-management view for fraud analysts and KYC operations teams. Track every case, filter by priority, monitor daily KPIs, and act fast on the highest-risk alerts.

### 👤 Role-Based Access
Built for teams. Analysts, KYC leads, and managers each get a personalized profile with the right level of access to cases and tools.

1. **Splash & Sign-in** — Secure login for analysts and KYC operators

3. **Risk Result Screen** — Detailed fraud signals, linked accounts, and recommended actions (escalate, verify, or block)
